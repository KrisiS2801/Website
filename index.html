Welcome

The Code Society Club at the American College of Sofia is made up of students with an interest towards informatics. The Club is full of enthusiastic personalities, helped by the occassional pizza party. We have our doors wide open towards any new members willing to join in. No amount of skill is required, only the desire to learn. And, needless to say, you'll enjoy the ride. We work on many different group projects, such as: video games, websites, a programing language, and possibly others in the future. Personal projects are also encouraged. If you want to enter a competition, you'll receive support of many kinds from the community here.
